## 2. What Data We Collect

### 2.1 Account Data
- Email address
- Password (stored as a cryptographic hash — we never see your actual password)
- Phone number (optional)
- Account creation date

### 2.2 Style Profile Data
- Skin tone and undertone (warm, cool, neutral, olive)
- Skin depth (light, medium, deep)
- Color season (Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter)
- Preferred clothing fit
- Style vibe and occasion preferences
- Region and location (country/city level)
- Gender

### 2.3 Preference Data
- Blacklisted materials (fabrics you've told us to avoid)
- Preferred materials (fabrics you prefer)
- Cosmetic preferences (foundation shade, coverage preference, etc.)

### 2.4 Usage Data
- Clothing items you enter for styling
- Recommendations generated for you
- Your feedback on recommendations (helpful / not helpful)
- Number of queries used per month
- Query timestamps

### 2.5 Location Data (Optional)
- Approximate GPS coordinates — only when you use weather-aware recommendations
- We request location permission in your browser
- We do not store your precise location — only weather data fetched from it
- You can deny location permission without losing access to the app

### 2.6 Technical Data
- IP address (logged by our infrastructure, not stored by us)
- Browser type and version
- Device type (desktop/mobile)
- Session tokens

### 2.7 What We Do NOT Collect
- Photos or images of you
- Your actual wardrobe inventory (unless you add it)
- Payment card details (handled entirely by our payment processor)
- Social media data
- Contact lists or address books

## 3. How We Use Your Data

### 3.1 To Provide the Service
- Generate personalized outfit recommendations using Claude AI (Anthropic)
- Apply your skin tone, fit preferences, and material rules to every recommendation
- Adjust recommendations based on your regional and cultural context
- Track your query usage against your plan limits

### 3.2 To Improve the Service
- Analyze which recommendation types users find helpful vs unhelpful
- Identify patterns in fashion rules that need refinement
- Monitor system performance and fix errors

### 3.3 AI Model Improvement (Opt-Out Available)
We may use anonymized, aggregated data to improve our styling engine.

**What this means specifically:**
- We may analyze patterns like "users with warm undertones in India tend to prefer these color combinations"
- We do NOT use your name, email, or any identifying information for this purpose
- We do NOT share your individual data with Anthropic or any AI company
- Anonymization means your data is stripped of all identifying attributes before any analysis
- You can opt out of this entirely — see Section 8

### 3.4 To Communicate With You
- Transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset)
- Service notifications (query limit reached, plan changes)
- We do not send marketing emails unless you explicitly opt in

### 3.5 Legal Compliance
- Comply with applicable laws
- Respond to lawful requests from authorities
- Protect our legal rights

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, we process your data under the following legal bases:

| Processing Activity | Legal Basis |
|---|---|
| Account creation and authentication | Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) |
| Generating style recommendations | Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) |
| Storing style profile and preferences | Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) |
| Location-based weather data | Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) |
| Service improvement analytics | Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) |
| AI training on anonymized data | Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) |
| Legal compliance | Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)) |

Special category data note: Skin tone information may constitute biometric or health-related data under GDPR Article 9. We process this data solely for the purpose of providing the styling service you have explicitly requested, under Art. 9(2)(a) (explicit consent provided at signup).

## 5. Who We Share Your Data With

### 5.1 Service Providers
We share data with the following third parties to operate the service:

| Provider | Purpose | Location | Data Shared |
|---|---|---|---|
| Supabase Inc. | Database and authentication | Germany (EU) | All user data |
| Anthropic PBC | AI recommendation engine | United States | Item description + anonymized profile |
| Lemon Squeezy | Payment processing | United States | Email, payment details |
| Open-Meteo | Weather data | Switzerland | GPS coordinates (temporary) |

### 5.2 What We Send to Anthropic (Claude API)
When you request a recommendation, we send:
- Item details (color, fabric, fit, type)
- Anonymized style profile (undertone, fit preference, region)
- Blacklisted and preferred materials
- Weather context (temperature, humidity)

We do NOT send: your name, email, user ID, or any identifying information to Anthropic.

### 5.3 We Do Not Sell Your Data
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for any purpose.

### 5.4 Business Transfers
If Assortir is acquired or merges with another company, your data may be transferred. We will notify you by email at least 30 days before any such transfer.

## 7. Your Rights

### 7.1 For All Users
- **Access**: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
- **Correction**: Update incorrect data in your profile settings
- **Deletion**: Delete your account and all associated data
- **Portability**: Request your data in a machine-readable format

### 7.2 Additional Rights for EU Users (GDPR)
- **Restriction**: Request we stop processing your data while a dispute is resolved
- **Objection**: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw consent for location data or AI training at any time
- **Lodge Complaint**: File a complaint with your national supervisory authority

### 7.3 For Indian Users (DPDP Act 2023)
- **Correction and Erasure**: As provided under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023
- **Grievance Redressal**: Contact our grievance officer at contact@assortir.app

### 7.4 How to Exercise Your Rights
Email us at contact@assortir.app with subject line "Data Request — [Your Right]"
We will respond within 30 days. We may ask you to verify your identity before processing the request.

## 8. AI Training Opt-Out

We may use anonymized and aggregated usage data to improve our styling recommendations.

**To opt out:**
1. Go to Settings in the app
2. Scroll to "Privacy"
3. Toggle off "Contribute to AI improvement"

Opting out does not affect your use of the service in any way.

## 10. Data Security

- All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3)
- All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256)
- Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we cannot read them
- API keys are stored in encrypted environment variables
- We use Row Level Security (RLS) — users can only access their own data
- Regular security reviews of our infrastructure
